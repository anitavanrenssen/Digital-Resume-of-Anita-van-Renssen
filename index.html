Dentistry has shown not to be that way in my case. I have always been intrigued by graphic design, and I always catch myself thinking about how to improve the UI/UX of a design. Being the designer as well as creator behind a great product is the dream. I am a 28 year old career changer, which shows that I am serious about entering the development and design industry and giving my all to make a success of it.

My greatest passion is wildlife, landscape and astrophotography. I have a great love for travelling our beautiful country in search of that perfect photo opportunity. Adventure always calls me and when I am not in the bush, you will find me on an overnight hiking trail in the mountains. I love playing and watching soccer, and I also appreciate a good book.
